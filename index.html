BOTW Database Browser

An application that allows the user to browse through a database of all creatures, monsters, materials, equipment, and treasure in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild! Simply click the category you are interested in, enter a name, and search!

Demacia vs. Noxus

A stylized version of tic-tac-toe stylized using key members of the League of Legends lore! Choose a team, and enjoy facing against your arch-enemy in this strategy game!

NASA Photo Browser

An application that allows the user to access NASA's public APIs in order to view their photos of the day. Just pick a date and enjoy looking at and learning about the various photos of the day carefully choose by NASA!
